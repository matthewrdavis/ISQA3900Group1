About Us

Logos Library is a public library that delivers e-content to members. Membership is free for users living in the United States, and allows you to check out items for 2 weeks at a time. Because Logos Library delivers content electronically, items are automatically returned on their due date. This means Logos Library does not charge late fees to its members.
